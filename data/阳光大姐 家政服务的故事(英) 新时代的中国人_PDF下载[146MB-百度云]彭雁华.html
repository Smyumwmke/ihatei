内容简介

[

本书讲述了一个家政服务公司“阳光大姐”群体的故事。“阳光大姐”是一家什么样的企业，怎么会得到国家领导人的特别关注？家政服务公司“阳光大姐”怎样创立起来的？在家政服务工作过程中，她们遇到过哪些不同的客户，碰到哪些具体形形色色问题？令人为难到什么程度？公司家政服务员如何热情地投入工作，以平静、坦然、乐观和高尚去克服种种难题？ Jinan Sunshine Sisters Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to
as “Sunshine Sisters” or “the Company”) is a market-driven
operational company specializing in housekeeping services.
It is a large-scale housekeeping company with more than
50,000 employees. As the largest housekeeping service
company in China, it developed under the leadership of
Zhuo Changli, a woman who was made redundant from the
Jinan Printing and Dyeing Mill in the early 1990s.

]
