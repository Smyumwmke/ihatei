关于本书
    雕塑园林是指在园林规划设计中应用雕塑艺术的创作理念．
将园林看作是一个大型的“雕塑”。在对园林造景材料的“雕”
与“塑”的过程中．实现设计理念的完整表达．并使科学性与
艺术性完美结合。    、．

体现了艺术家李险峰“大雕塑”的观念．即雕塑与园林环境是
有机的整体．雕塑服从于园林的整体规划设计，园林环境为雕
塑营造艺术氛围．两者共同构成一件完美的景观艺术品。

点．再现了景观体量关系：用精美的图片展现了景区建成后美
丽的风光。

About The Book
 "Sculptural Landscape Architecture" refers to the application of the
creation concept of sculpture in landscape architecture,which regards
landscape as a large-scale "sculpture".Through the "sculpturing"process
of the landscape,the author's creation idea is completely expressed, and
the artistry and the scientificalness are perfectly combined.
The Nanhai Lake Project is a classic of integrated designs of sculpture
and landscape, which reflects artist Xianfeng Li's "Large-scale Sculpture"
concept, i.e. the sculpture and the landscape is an inseparable whole; the
design of the sculpture must be in harmony with the landscape, and the
landscape creates artistic atmosphere for the sculpture.
The author uses poetic language to interpret the design concept of Li and
his team's Nanhai Lake Project. The key concept of the design is
elaborated statistically and the beauty of the completed landscape
architecture is showed by exquisite pictures.
The author hopes to communicate his ideas with artists and professionals
through the publication of this book.
